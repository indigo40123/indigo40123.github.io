Teaching

Beside of my research project, I am very passionate about teaching science. It is truly satisfying to be able to help others understand the key concept and get them to enjoy the beauty of science. Also, teaching often is a great opportunity to review the exist knowledge and think about the overall picture.

As a student of the teaching education program since my junior year of undergrad at NTNU, I have had a lot of opportunities to teach other students physics. During my senior year, I ran the physics courses through the The Affiliated Senior High School of National Taiwan Normal University(HSNU). Also, I have took a half-year internship in Taichung Municipal Taichung First Senior High School(TCFSH).
